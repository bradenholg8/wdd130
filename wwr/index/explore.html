Green River

Discover the thrill of rafting one of America's Top 10 Rivers. Much of Green River carves through the Colorado Plateau in Southern Utah, boasting some of the most spectacular canyons in the United States.

Colorado River

The mighty Colorado River has eroded an intricate system of canyons and towering mesas. This is a wilderness rich in natural wonder and big whitewater. Cataract Canyon Rafting trips begin on a tranquil meandering desert river. The Colorado River passes spectacular side canyons, archeological sites, pristine beaches, and diverse ecological communities. Rafting the Colorado is an experience like no other.

Snake River

On this relaxing rafting trip from Jackson Hole, drift down a scenic section of Wyoming's wildlife-laden Snake River, where moose, deer, river otters and eagles can all be seen from your raft. Be sure to bring your camera to photograph any wildlife that appears on the banks, and also capture the stunning landscape extending out from the Tetons.
