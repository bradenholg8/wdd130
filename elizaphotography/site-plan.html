Content

Home page

My site will be a place to hire someone (my sister, Eliza) to help capture special moments in their lives. We all have cameras on our phones, but there is something special that comes by hiring a professional photographer to capture the memories to remember. Many special opportunities include family pictures, pictures of pets, of children, pictures of landscapes and nature, misc pictures that peek your curiousity, and vacations. Photos of special events are the most common. These include wedding days, receptions, Mission Callings and Farewells, baby blessings, pregnancy announcements, gender reveals, birthdays, or even days when you finally have the rare moments to have the entire family together and want an updated large family photo for the front room of your home.

Testimonials

This page is the place to see the reviews of previous clients that hired Eliza for photo sessions. With access to this page, people can begin to gain excitement about finding someone to assist them to save their special memories.
